Place Preservation Period
Refrigerator 1 week
Freezer 2 weeks
Preservation Method
Method 1
After washing and drying the leaves (cutting big leaves like romaine down is up to you at this point), the lettuce is laid out on paper towels, then rolled up and placed in a plastic bag.
Method 2
After washing and drying the leaves (cutting big leaves like romaine down is up to you at this point), the lettuce is placed in a plastic bag. The air should be left out.
